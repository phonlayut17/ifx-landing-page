Pricing is calculated based on the active customer(drop-point) in the system. The cost is summarized monthly on the 1st day, using the maximum number of active customer(drop-point) in that previous month. Service fee will automatically deduct from your credit-card on every 16th of month.

For example; Factory A has maximum active customers in July 100 drop-point. 1st August, the service fee will be calculated as below.
active customer * package price* day * VAT7%* Service charge 3.65% = service fee 100*0.38THB*31*1.07*1.0365 = 1,306.46 THB
